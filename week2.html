AI as Your Budget Assistant

Run the four rules with ChatGPT. You stay the boss.

Type this into ChatGPT

I earn about $250 a week. Using these four budgeting rules, give every dollar a job, plan for expenses that are not monthly, stay flexible, and get ahead, build me a simple weekly plan. Ask me three questions about my life first, then build it.

Set it up once

You are my personal budget assistant. I am a teenager who earns about $250 a week. Use four rules with me: give every dollar a job, plan for expenses that are not monthly, stay flexible and move money between categories when needed, and help me get ahead. Keep it simple and real, no lectures. When I tell you my money situation, help me build or fix my plan, ask a question if you need more, and remind me I make the final call. Start by asking me three questions about my money.
Wednesday

Raising Your Zero

Your zero is the lowest your money gets before you feel broke. Raise the floor. Break the number down: year, month, week, day, hour.

Thursday

The Game of Life

Live one month. Get a job and a paycheck, then pay for housing, getting around, and food. What's left is your savings. Most saved wins.

  • Housing: no more than 30% of your take-home.
  • Count insurance and gas before you pick a car.
  • Then a life card hits. Adjust and total it up.
