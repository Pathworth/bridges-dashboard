Wednesday

The Four Rules

The four rules are YNAB's, and the names are exact. Do not paraphrase them.

  • Rule 1: Give Every Dollar a Job. Every dollar you have gets assigned somewhere before you spend it.
  • Rule 2: Embrace Your True Expenses. The big bills that are not monthly still come. Break them down and save for them a little at a time.
  • Rule 3: Roll with the Punches. When you overspend somewhere, move money from somewhere else. Adjust, do not quit.
  • Rule 4: Age Your Money. Spend money you earned a while ago, not the check that just hit. That is how you stop living paycheck to paycheck.

Raising Your Zero

Your zero is the lowest your money gets before you feel broke. Raise the floor. Break the number down: year, month, week, day, hour.

Thursday

The Game of Life

Live one month. Get a job and a paycheck, then pay for housing, getting around, and food. What's left is your savings. Most saved wins.

  • Housing: no more than 30% of your take-home.
  • Count insurance and gas before you pick a car.
  • Then a life card hits. Adjust and total it up.
